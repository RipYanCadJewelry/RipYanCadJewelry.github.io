How it works

From Concept to Print

I

Brief & Reference

Share your sketch, photo, or description. I confirm dimensions, stone sizes, and material finish before starting.

II

CAD Modeling

Precision 3D modeling in Rhino / ZBrush with production-accurate tolerances and assembly logic.

III

Review & Revisions

Renders sent for approval. Up to two revision rounds included. Final file validated for printability.

IV

File Delivery

STL, OBJ, and source files delivered. Ready for SLA/DLP printing or direct CNC milling.
